About Us

Welcome to Mexer. Our mission is to empower researchers to better understand energy conversion chains (ECCs) through intuitive visualization tools underpinned by the PSUT framework.

Our Vision

We envision a sustainable world in which everyone has access to clear and actionable energy data. By providing this web application, we aim to make understanding and working with energy data easier, fostering informed, sustainable policy-making.

Our Team

Our team consists of varied and passionate people, with the common goal of a sustainable world. From engineers to economists, the Energy Economics Team is dedicated to the research and presentation of sustainable policies.
